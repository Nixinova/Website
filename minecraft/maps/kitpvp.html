KitPvP

Play the popular minigame KitPvP in vanilla Minecraft.
Pick one of the eight kits and fight to the death!
In this version, there are two modes – singleplayer and multiplayer.
Singleplayer lets you battle naturally-spawned mobs and multiplayer lets you fight other players.

What is KitPvP?

KitPvP is a popular minigame where each player chooses one of the ten kits
– Warrior, Knight, Archer, Mage, Witch, Nurse, Caveman, Fisherman, Brute, YOLO –
and fight other players in an unending PvP match!
