SkyWars

A fully-functional SkyWars map in vanilla Minecraft, made for my 200 subscriber special!
Supports up to 12 people. Based off of Hypixel Skywars.

What is SkyWars?

SkyWars is a 12-player PvP game where players spawn on separate islands, gather loot, and fight other players in a bid to be the last one standing.
There is a larger island in the center of the map which contains better loot that you would find on your spawn island.
