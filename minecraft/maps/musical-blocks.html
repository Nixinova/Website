Musical Blocks

The minigame Musical Blocks or Block Party recreated in vanilla Minecraft.
Only works in Minecraft 1.8.0–1.8.5.

What is Musical Blocks?

Musical Blocks is a small minigame where the player has to run to whatever colour the map tells them to go to.
After a few seconds the ground not of that colour will disappear and any players on them will fall to their deaths.
The winner is the last player remaining.
