KitPvP

Play the popular minigame KitPvP in vanilla Minecraft. Pick one of the 10 kits and fight to the death! In this version, there are two modes – singleplayer and multiplayer. Singleplayer lets you battle naturally-spawned mobs and multiplayer lets you fight other players.

What is KitPvP?

KitPvP is a popular minigame in which each player chooses a premade kit – in this case, Caveman, Witch, Nurse, YOLO, Knight, Mage, Archer, Warrior, Fisherman, or Brute – and fight other players in a continuous PvP or PvE battle arena!
